What it does
The prompter your audience can't see

Invisible to every recorder

eyeread renders outside the OS capture layer. Zoom, Loom, QuickTime, OBS — none of them see it. Your audience never knows it's there.

Voice tracking

It listens. The highlight follows the word you're saying, not a timer. Pause for emphasis — it waits. Speed up — it keeps up.

Near the camera

Anchors at the top of your screen, just below the webcam. Your eyes stay on the lens — not drifting down to notes. You look present.

Works on any device

macOS, Windows, any browser — eyeread adapts. Overlay your slides, your call, your recording setup. Any content, any tool.

Timer built in

Count up from first word, or count down to a target. Stay on time without looking at your watch. One less thing to manage.

Free, forever

No trial, no paywall, no "pro" tier. MIT licensed. The full app is yours. If it helps you, donate. If not, it's still yours.

Open source · MIT license
Built in the open. Kept free on purpose.

eyeread.in is fully open source. Read the code, audit it, fork it, contribute to it. No tracking, no telemetry, no subscription hidden in plain sight. The people who find it genuinely useful keep it alive by donating — voluntarily, without pressure.
