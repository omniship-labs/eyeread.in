Naming · round 3 · mark explorations
Mark directions + more names
01 — The mark: three directions
A · The script panel
oncue
Contained, professional. Reads well at any size. The glowing line = the cue you're on.
B · The camera lens
speakeasy
The camera aperture + the cue line inside. Says "eye on the lens." More photographic.
C · The naked caret
offbook
No container — just the floating line in space. Works perfectly on dark UI. Minimal and confident.
