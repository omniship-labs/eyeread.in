01 — mark directions (hover each)
A
Script panel
Contained, friendly, scales from favicon to billboard
B
Camera lens
The aperture = your eye + the lens you look into
C
The eye
Your gaze + the camera. The line = what you're reading

Naked caret
No container — just the floating line. Ultra-minimal, most confident

Waveform cue
Voice detection bars + the cue line cutting through

Iris aperture
Camera iris blades = focus, precision, the moment you go on
G
Whisper bubble
Reversed tail = whispering TO the speaker. The prompter speaks first

Viewfinder
Camera composition frame. You're always "in frame" with the prompter

Cursor / I-beam
You are here. The live text cursor mid-sentence. Very literal, very clean
J
Scan line
CRT horizontal scan — cinematic, technical, very teleprompter-specific
K
Mirror / reflect
The beam-splitter is a half-mirror. Script reflects to the speaker, invisible to camera
L
Signal tower
On-air broadcast signal. You're live — and invisible to the feed
M
Ripple / radar
Concentric rings = signal rippling out. Focus rings in on your active line
N
Crosshair / aim
Lock on. You're precisely where you need to be. Precision + focus
O
Scroll
The ancient teleprompter. Literal and timeless — speakers have used scrolls forever
P
Magnifier / focus
Zoom in on your cue. Precision focus — see every word clearly
Q
Spotlight
The prompter shines on only you — and only you can see it
R
Signal bars
You're at full signal, fully on-air. Growing bars = building confidence
S
Split / invisible line
Two worlds divided. The audience sees one; you see both. The dotted line = the split
T
Monogram C
A glowing "C" for Cue — pure lettermark. Works at 16px and 16 feet
