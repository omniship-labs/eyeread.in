What makes it different

Built for how people actually present.

Invisible to every recorder

eyeread.in renders outside the captured frame. Zoom, Loom, OBS, QuickTime — none of them see it. Your audience only sees the content.

Screen-share safe

Voice tracking

Speak, and the script follows. The current word glows. What you've said fades. No tapping, no foot pedal — just talk.

Near the camera

The overlay anchors just below your webcam. Your eyes naturally land on the lens — not the corner of the screen. You look present.

How it works

Open it. Paste your script. Talk.

1

Paste or type your script

Drop in anything — a keynote, a YouTube script, talking points, interview answers. eyeread.in works with plain text.

2

Position the overlay

Drag it near your camera. Adjust font size, opacity, and speed. It floats above every other window.

3

Start talking

Voice detection takes over. The active word is highlighted. The text scrolls as you speak. Hit record whenever you're ready.
