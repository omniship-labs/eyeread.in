Compact status pill (mono uppercase). The `record` tone pulses its dot.

```jsx
<Badge tone="accent" dot>Live</Badge>
<Badge tone="record" dot>Rec</Badge>
<Badge tone="success" dot>Synced</Badge>
<Badge tone="neutral" solid>Draft</Badge>
```

- **dot** adds a leading status dot. **solid** switches to sentence-case sans (for counts/labels).
