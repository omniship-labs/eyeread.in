About
eyeread.in is an open source teleprompter for macOS. It floats your script as a glass overlay over whatever you're presenting — slides, a Zoom call, a recording — and stays completely invisible to every screen recorder. Zoom, Loom, OBS, QuickTime: none of them see it. Voice detection tracks what you're saying and highlights the active line in real time. Your eyes stay on the camera, not on your notes. Free. Open source. AGPL-3.0. → eyeread.in

Description
Most teleprompters are obvious. Your audience can tell you're reading. eyeread.in floats your script as a glass overlay on top of whatever you're sharing — slides, a Zoom call, a recording. Zoom, Loom, OBS, QuickTime — none of them capture it. Your audience sees a clean presentation. You see your words. Voice detection listens as you talk. The active line highlights automatically. Look up, not down. Free. Open source. AGPL-3.0.
